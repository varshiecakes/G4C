PITCH

Help us Out, is a game presented after an earthquake near an apartment building in San Diego, houses have been victim to structral decstruction.. After the devasting event peoples precious belongings are gone! We will have to help find the lost objects. You may not have any money as a ghost, but giving a helping hand to people in need can never hurt!

THEME

This game is set in a surburban area, with some but little trees, and an apartment building (one of which is crumbling down), I wanted to provide a chilly feel in a sad time in the city, sort of taking place during winter.

MY WHY

I wanted to show how devasting earthquakes can be, since people in my family have lost valuable things to them from a natural disaster.
